Senior Data Analyst — GreenLeaf Commerce (Remote, US)

About the role:
We're hiring a Senior Data Analyst to own analytics for our growing e-commerce business.
You'll partner with marketing, product, and finance to turn data into decisions, build
self-serve dashboards, and run experiments that move key metrics.

What you'll do:
- Build and maintain dashboards in Looker for stakeholders across the company.
- Design and analyze A/B tests to improve conversion and retention.
- Write and optimize SQL against our Snowflake warehouse; model data in dbt.
- Translate ambiguous business questions into clear analyses and recommendations.
- Mentor junior analysts and raise the bar on analytical rigor.

What we're looking for:
- 4+ years in data/analytics, ideally in e-commerce or retail.
- Strong SQL and experience with a cloud data warehouse (Snowflake preferred).
- Proficiency with a BI tool (Looker preferred) and Python for analysis.
- Hands-on A/B testing and experimentation experience.
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills.
- Bonus: dbt, forecasting, or statistics background.
